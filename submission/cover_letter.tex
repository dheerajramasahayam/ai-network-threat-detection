\noindent Dear Editor,

\vspace{1em}

I am submitting the manuscript titled \textit{Drift-Adaptive Intrusion Detection for Enterprise Networks} for consideration in \textit{IEEE Transactions on Network and Service Management}.

This paper presents a reproducible enterprise intrusion-detection study that combines four public benchmarks, a transparent flow-signature IDS baseline, three established learning models, a static hybrid stack, and an online drift-adaptive controller. The manuscript is positioned as a networked systems and operational security paper: it evaluates full official splits for UNSW-NB15 and NSL-KDD, full-corpus external transfer into CICIDS2017, second external validation on CSE-CIC-IDS2018, a formal drift-detector comparison, real-time streaming behavior, failure-case analysis, and a packet-replay case study collected from a controlled internal lab environment simulating enterprise traffic.

The work is original, is not under review elsewhere, and has not been previously published in archival form. All figures, tables, and experimental claims are backed by the repository artifacts included with the submission package. The manuscript also contains an explicit conflict-of-interest disclosure stating that the author has no conflicts of interest to declare.

I believe the paper fits the journal because it addresses enterprise network monitoring, deployment-oriented intrusion detection, streaming adaptation under distribution shift, and reproducible evaluation across multiple operational settings.
